Meta AI: **Evaluation Metrics**

Conversational Artificial Intelligence (CAI) systems are evaluated using a range of metrics to assess their performance and effectiveness. These metrics can be broadly categorized into:

### 1. Perplexity

Perplexity measures the uncertainty of a model's predictions. A lower perplexity indicates better performance.

Perplexity (PP) = 2^(-∑(p(x) \* log2(p(x))))

where p(x) is the probability of the predicted response.

### 2. BLEU Score

BLEU (Bilingual Evaluation Understudy) score measures the similarity between the predicted response and the reference response.

BLEU Score = (n-gram precision)^(1/n) \* (e^(-1/e))^(1-e)

where n is the number of grams, precision is the precision of the n-gram, and e is the exposure.

### 3. ROUGE Score

ROUGE (Recall-Oriented Understudy for Gisting Evaluation) score measures the similarity between the predicted response and the reference response.

ROUGE Score = (n-gram recall)^(1/n) \* (e^(-1/e))^(1-e)

where n is the number of grams, recall is the recall of the n-gram, and e is the exposure.

### 4. METEOR Score

METEOR (Metric for Evaluation of Translation with Explicit ORdering) score measures the similarity between the predicted response and the reference response.

METEOR Score = (Precision \* Recall) / (0.5 \* Precision + 0.5 \* Recall)

where Precision and Recall are the precision and recall of the predicted response.

### 5. Human Evaluation

Human evaluation involves assessing the quality and coherence of the predicted responses.

**Comparison Table:**
